ABOUT FULL BELLY FARM

Since 2014, Full Belly Farm Sanctuary has grown to be home to 27 mini pigs, 4 goats, 2 sheep, a donkey, 4 dogs, chickens, ducks, a rooster, and a turkey! Many of our rescued or fostered animal friends have since found their forever homes all over the country.

Initially, the focus was on rescuing and fostering shelter dogs and cats. Eventually, a lifelong dream of raising a miniature pig became a reality. Petunia came along in 2014 and was the start of Full Belly Farm Animal Sanctuary!

Full Belly Farm Animal Sanctuary is a 501c3 nonprofit animal rescue located in Lexington, AL. Our mission is to rescue and provide sanctuary for unwanted animals, with the focus on miniature pigs, and advocate for spaying and neutering.
